About Us

Experienced Chess Instructors

We are the Windsor Chess Lions, dedicated to promoting the ancient game of strategy and intellect across Canada and the USA. Starting out as a community chess club in 2016, we kept growing. We've proudly coached, taught, and led Team Windsor (ON) and Team Novi (MI) teams to victory in several state-level championships. With us, everyone can be a champion.
